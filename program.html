Programmes

The School offers the following academic programs:


  • Bachelor of Technology in computer science and engineering (4-year undergraduate program)

    • The B.Tech. program is a four-year course oriented undergraduate program. The course work is spread accross all the semesters. The courses include a set of core courses offered by the department, a set of departmental electives and some free electives. Apart from this, a student must complete three courses in his or her minor area. The minor area must be different from computer science. Besides, a student must also complete a project in the eighth semester towards the fulfillment of the degree requirements.


  • Bachelor of Technology in electrical engineering (4-year undergraduate program)

    • The Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Mandi, which has a fine blend of renowned as well as young and dynamic personalities as faculty, is involved in providing quality education at Undergraduate (UG). The syllabi of the courses are continuously updated and the laboratories modernized to reflect the rapid changes in technology.


  • Master of Science by Research (research thesis-based graduate degree program)

    • Master of Science (M.S.) by Research programme at IIT Mandi is designed to help scholars acquire in-depth knowledge by carrying out advanced research in a chosen area. As compared to the M. Tech. programme, M.S. is research oriented and demands a thesis that is a significant contribution to the area of research. In addition to the thesis students have to complete five courses related to their area of research.


  • Doctor of Philosophy (research thesis-based doctoral degree program)
